Analysis

Shrek goes on a quest to get his swamp back, along the way he is aided by puss in boots, and the muffin man. Fairy godmother(charmings mother) and Prince Charming make his life hard. Donkey and fiona help him along the way, but shrek is mean to Fiona, but later attempts to marry her. Shreks most hated time was associating with all the fairytail creatures. He quickly delivered Fiona to Farquad so they could be married, but stops their wedding in time to marry her instead. At the end Shrek takes Fiona home and they have many adventures together.
